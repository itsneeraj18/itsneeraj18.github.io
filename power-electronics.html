‚ö° Overview: Power Electronics in EVs

Power electronics bridges the gap between the battery (DC source) and the motor (AC load) through high-efficiency conversion and control. Modern EV inverters achieve 95%+ efficiency, minimizing energy waste and extending vehicle range.

Beyond motor drive, power electronics handle on-board charging, auxiliary power distribution, and regenerative braking‚Äîmaking it a critical subsystem for EV performance and reliability.

Key Power Electronics Subsystems

  • Main Inverter: High-power DC-AC conversion for motor drive
  • On-Board Charger (OBC): AC-DC conversion for battery charging
  • DC-DC Converter: Voltage step-down for 12V auxiliary systems
  • Gate Drivers: PWM signal generation and power device switching
  • Thermal Management: Cooling and temperature regulation
