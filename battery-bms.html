The battery system is the heart of any electric vehicle, storing and delivering electrical energy to power the motor. Modern EV batteries use lithium-ion chemistry for their high energy density, efficiency, and mature manufacturing processes.

Battery management systems (BMS) are equally critical, ensuring cell safety, optimizing state-of-charge estimation, and maximizing battery lifespan through active monitoring and thermal management.

Battery System Components

  • Cells: Individual lithium-ion chemistry units
  • Modules: Grouped cells with local management
  • Pack: Complete battery system with BMS and cooling
  • BMS: Electronics for monitoring, balancing, and protection
  • Thermal System: Liquid or air cooling for optimal temperatures
